# Pinlytics

Pinlytics is a bowling analytics app built to turn your games into clear, useful insights — faster tracking, deeper stats, and better decisions between frames.

## What you can do with Pinlytics
- Track games and performance over time
- Upload scorecards (AI extraction) to save time
- Explore stats and trends to improve faster
- Manage leagues, goals, and equipment
