# Our Services
NV Data Technologies LLC develops and operates digital software products, with a focus on data-driven mobile and web applications. Our work includes the creation, management, and improvement of consumer-facing apps—such as Pinlytics, our bowling analytics platform—and related digital services. We build, maintain, and support applications available on major platforms including iOS, Android, and the web.
